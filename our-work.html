Our Work

ANNUAL LEADERSHIP LECTURE

The purpose of the lecture is to espouse the values of leadership anchored on integrity, honesty and accountability. These are values that Chief Akande has been known for in his public and political career. The lecture shall focus on any aspect of leadership, and should be delivered by a known Nigerian with clear manifestations of the ideals of leadership.

ANNUAL ROUND-TABLE ON FOOD SECURITY

With rising food scarcity and rise in food prices in the country, food security is no doubt one of the issues on the front burner of our national discussion, and it will be in order for the AAF to lend its voice to this, and suggest actionable programmes for National and State Governments. Conclusions and recommendations from the round table, in the form of policy briefs, should be communicated to the relevant government ministries and agencies. The round table will comprise an academic in the relevant area as moderator, and 5 stakeholders as discussants.

EMERGING LEADERS FELLOWSHIP

The AAF's Emerging Leaders Fellowship is a prestigious, non-partisan leadership training program for young Nigerians between the ages of 21 and 35. Each cohort of this fellowship seeks to empower high-potential Nigerians who are expected to ascend to leadership roles in the public sector and other key spheres over the next decade. Participants will be trained to embrace the virtues of Transparency, Efficiency, and Accountability (TEA), which are the cornerstones of Chief Bisi Akande’s leadership legacy. The program will be administered through the Adebisi Akande Leadership Institute being developed at the Igbinedion University, Okada (IUO), Edo State.
